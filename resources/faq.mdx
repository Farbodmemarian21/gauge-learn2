### Why isn't my brand appearing in AI responses?

There are a few common reasons:
- Your brand may not yet have enough authoritative, citable content for AI engines to pull from
- The prompts you're tracking may not be closely aligned with the content you've published
- Competitors may have significantly stronger citation footprints for those queries

The Action Center in Gauge will surface specific recommendations to address these gaps.

---

### How is AEO different from SEO?

SEO optimizes for ranking in a list of links on a traditional search engine results page. AEO optimizes for being mentioned, cited, or recommended within an AI-generated conversational answer. The metrics, signals, and content strategies are different — though strong SEO often supports strong AEO.

### Can I track competitors in Gauge?

Yes. You can add competitor brands to your Gauge workspace and track their visibility, rankings, and citations alongside your own. This benchmarking data helps you understand where you're leading or falling behind.

### What should I do if AI engines are saying something inaccurate about my brand?

Gauge will surface inaccurate AI responses in your Branded Prompts view. The most effective remediation strategy is to publish clear, authoritative content that directly addresses the inaccuracy — AI engines tend to update their responses as they encounter more accurate, well-cited sources.

---

### How many prompts should I track?

We recommend starting with at least 10–20 prompts that cover a mix of:
- Industry/problem-focused queries ("best tools for X")
- Comparison queries ("[Brand] vs [Competitor]")
- Direct branded queries ("What is [Brand]?")
- Use-case queries ("How to do X")

More prompts give you a more complete picture of your AEO performance.

---

### Does Gauge integrate with Slack?

Yes. You can connect Gauge to a Slack channel to receive automated alerts and weekly digests. Set this up in **Settings → Integrations**.
