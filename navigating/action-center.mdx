- Types of content actions: create, update, optimize
- Prioritizing actions by potential visibility impact
- Tracking which actions you've completed

[Explain the Action Center as Gauge's recommendation engine — it analyzes your current visibility, citation gaps, and competitor performance to surface specific content actions your team can take to improve AEO performance.]

[Describe the different types of actions Gauge surfaces:

### Prioritizing by Impact

[Explain how Gauge scores recommendations by potential visibility impact — high-impact actions are those where a competitor has a strong citation foothold and you have none. Walk through how to sort and filter by impact score.]
