## Summary

<Note>
  This lesson covers the Rankings & Visibility dashboard — how to interpret your scores, track changes over time, and compare your performance against competitors.
</Note>

**Key Concepts:**
- Visibility score: percentage of tracked prompts where your brand appears
- Rankings: your brand's position relative to competitors in AI responses
- Trends over time: how your scores change week over week
- Competitor benchmarking: how you stack up against other brands
