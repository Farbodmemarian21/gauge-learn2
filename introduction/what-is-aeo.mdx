## Summary

<Note>
  This lesson explains what AEO is, how it differs from traditional SEO, and why it matters for your brand right now.
</Note>

**Key Concepts:**
- AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimization
- AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) are changing how people find information
- Brands can be cited, mentioned, or ignored in AI-generated answers
- AEO is about optimizing your brand's presence in those answers

## Overview

### What is an Answer Engine?

[Explain what answer engines are — AI-powered tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google Gemini, and others that respond to user queries with synthesized answers rather than a list of links. These tools are rapidly becoming a primary way people research products, services, and brands.]

### How AEO differs from SEO

[Explain the key difference: SEO optimizes for ranking in a list of links on a search engine results page. AEO optimizes for being cited, mentioned, or recommended within an AI-generated answer. The metrics, signals, and strategies are different.]

### Why AEO matters now

[Explain the shift in user behavior — a growing percentage of searches are happening in AI tools rather than traditional search engines. Brands that are not tracking or optimizing their AI visibility are missing a significant and growing channel.]

### How AI engines decide what to say

[Explain at a high level how LLMs pull from training data, real-time web retrieval, and citations to generate answers. The content your brand publishes, the sources that mention you, and the prompts people use all influence whether and how you appear.]

<Warning>
  If you're not tracking your AEO performance, you have no visibility into whether AI tools are recommending, ignoring, or misrepresenting your brand.
</Warning>
